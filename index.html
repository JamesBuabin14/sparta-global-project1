Matching Cards



Matching Card Instructions

The aim of the game is for the player to pair up all the cards that are on the table.

All the cards have a face of a football logo and are positioned randomly on the table

If the player selects a two cards that do not match that will be flipped back

if the selected cards match they will remain displaying the face of the card until all the cards have been correctly paired

As the game is running the countdown is ticking! If the time runs its game over for the player

If the player finds all the cards they win!
